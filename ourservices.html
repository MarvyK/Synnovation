Our Services

Synnovation is a team of like-minded, independent

Innovation Catalysts

who facilitate creative change management process for organisation and companies

We are catalysts for creativity and innovation. Our team, our ways of working and our processes enable people to access their untapped creative genius, and apply it to their current world to get fresh perspectives and solutions to problems or challenges.

We use our same recipe, with your different ingredients to amplify critical and analytical thinking skills and combine them with creative thinking skills – in the right mix and right time, for the best results.

We use creativity to supercharge critical and analytical thinking to uncover fresh solutions and implement new ideas. It’s the kind of thinking that makes people more energised, positive, adaptive and resilient for change. The power of the approach is that it simultaneously addresses creativity, novel strategies, behavioural and people issues, and focussed innovation to deliver practical implementation.

SynNovation is a 20-year old majority Black and Female-owned company headquartered in South Africa, with a presence in Cape Town, Johannesburg as well as in Perth, Australia. We work with a team, whose diverse skills and backgrounds enable us to work locally, internationally, in-person or on-line and across multiple industries.

Consulting Services

CATALYST-LED CONSULTING

Our facilitators are catalysts for innovation. Our practice energises a culture where creativity and collaboration can flourish. We work alongside your team to identify the most important challenges and to use creative problem-solving techniques to unlock the potential of your people to solve those challenges. The outcomes are two-fold; delivering solutions and strategies for approaching new opportunities, as well teambuilding invigorated by working together in a pro-active, collaborative and fun way.

ORGANISATIONAL REVIEW

Designed to take stock of how your organisation, team or group is functioning. By examining what works currently and where there can be improvements, teams build solutions to solve challenges together.

What:
Participants:

  • Identify what is going well
  • Identify the best current thinking and solutions
  • Identify the challenges and opportunities
  • Work together to solve the most important current organisational challenges

Who:
Ideal for:

  • Testing how well systems are working
  • Introducing a change management programme
  • Getting buy-in for new systems or ideas
  • Managing conflict
  • Innovation
  • Energising and merging teams

When:
Duration is customised to the requirement and can be delivered in-person or online

ENERGISING INDIVIDUALS AND TEAMS

Leverage the skills of our catalysts to energise your team. The SynNovation groundrules and tools for creativity are an ideal way to kick off meetings, both online and in-person, building connection and bringing a human-centred approach to work.

What:
Participants:

  • Experience the behaviours that support collaboration
  • Use short tools to energise your team and kick-start creative thinking
  • Set a collaborative climate to spark renewed energy in meetings

Who:
Ideal for:

  • Kicking off online meetings with a distributed workforce Adding thinking partners for smaller teams Re-igniting enthusiasm for a project When: Duration is customised to the requirement and can be delivered in-person or online

CROSS-SECTORAL COLLABORATION

Collaborating with different stakeholders offers the opportunity for diverse inputs to strengthen the output of any project. Invite our impartial SynNovation catalysts to guide your teams to solve complex problems together. From large-scale conference break-outs to inter-departmental collaboration and multi-party projects, our ways of working inspire collaborative results in a productive way.

MULTIPLE STAKEHOLDER COLLABORATION

Designed to solve complex problems involving multiple stakeholders, often with opposing views, to find solutions together and increase long-term collaboration.

What:
Stakeholders work together to:

  • Identify the key stakeholders and challenges
  • Build empathy by uncovering gaps in single-stakeholder solutions
  • Approach the challenges from the perspective of each of the stakeholders to create win-win solutions
  • Break down silos

Who:
Particularly suited where there are different groups with agendas for example:

  • Community consultation as part of environmental impact study
  • Community consultation by municipalities
  • Multiple contractors working for a client

When:
Duration is customised to the requirement and can be delivered in-person or online.

CONNECTED CONFERENCES

Designed to set the theme of a conference and build a collaborative climate. Facilitator guided break-out group discussions at conferences can be turned into idea generation powerhouses to find solutions to delegates’ shared challenges.

What:
Conference participants use tools to:

  • Listen to content in new ways
  • Identify key tasks for action
  • Generate ideas
  • Develop concepts to put into action plan for report back after the conference

When:

  • Duration is customised to conference requirements, in-person or online
