About Us

We are catalysts for creativity and innovation.

Our team, our ways of working and our processes enable people to access their untapped creative genius, and apply it to their current world to get fresh perspectives and solutions to problems or challenges.

We use our recipe, with your ingredients to enliven your critical and analytical thinking skills and spice them up with creative thinking skills – in the right mix and right time, for the best results.

Our Services

Smart organisations know they must innovate and lead effectively to get – and stay – ahead of their competitors.

The highly-skilled SynNovation team has worked both internationally and locally, to help clients do just that. If you’re looking to build effective teams with thinking skills and solution mind-sets to help improve your profits, deliver better services and enjoy better operational efficiencies - we’re the right choice to partner with you.

With you, we develop sustainable long-term change and innovation through a custom-designed, end-to-end total innovation programme. As clients, you can opt to select elements of the tools, skills and strategies best suited to your requirements for short- or long-term involvement.
